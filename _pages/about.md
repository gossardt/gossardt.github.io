I am a PhD student in Robotics at the University of Tübingen under the supervision of Prof. [Andreas Zell](https://uni-tuebingen.de/fakultaeten/mathematisch-naturwissenschaftliche-fakultaet/fachbereiche/informatik/lehrstuehle/kognitive-systeme/the-chair/staff/prof-dr-andreas-zell/) and Prof. [Georg Martius](https://uni-tuebingen.de/fakultaeten/mathematisch-naturwissenschaftliche-fakultaet/fachbereiche/informatik/lehrstuehle/distributed-intelligence/team/prof-dr-georg-martius/), in collaboration with Sony AI as part of the [Ace project](https://ace.ai.sony/).
My research focused on enabling table tennis robots to understand and exploit spin, requiring advances in perception, physical modeling, and control.

I enjoy building complete robotic systems that bridge the gap between theory and deployment, combining rigorous modeling with practical engineering to solve challenging real-world problems.
